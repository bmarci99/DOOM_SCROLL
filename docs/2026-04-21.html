arXiv MultiWorld: Scalable Multi-Agent Multi-View Video World Models
2026-04-20 · 4 pts · cs.CV · Haoyu Wu, Jiwen Yu…
📄 New in cs.CV
Video world models have achieved remarkable success in simulating environmental dynamics in response to actions by users or agents. We present \textbf{MultiWorld}, a unified framework for multi-agent multi-view world modeling that enables accurate control of multiple agents while maintaining multi-view consistency. Experiments on multi-player game environments and multi-robot manipulation tasks demonstrate that MultiWorld outperforms baselines in video fidelity, action-following ability, and multi-view consistency.
arXiv ClawEnvKit: Automatic Environment Generation for Claw-Like Agents
2026-04-20 · 2 pts · cs.AI · cs.CL · Xirui Li, Ming Li…
📄 New in cs.AI, cs.CL
Constructing environments for training and evaluating claw-like agents remains a manual, human-intensive process that does not scale. Using ClawEnvKit, we construct Auto-ClawEval, the first large-scale benchmark for claw-like agents, comprising 1,040 environments across 24 categories. Beyond static benchmarking, ClawEnvKit enables live evaluation: users describe a desired capability in natural language and obtain a verified environment on demand, turning evaluation into a continuous, user-driven process.
