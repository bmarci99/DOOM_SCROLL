arXiv RAD-2: Scaling Reinforcement Learning in a Generator-Discriminator Framework
2026-04-16 · 201 pts · cs.CV · Hao Gao, Shaoyu Chen…
📄 New in cs.CV
High-level autonomous driving requires motion planners capable of modeling multimodal future uncertainties while remaining robust in closed-loop interactions. Although diffusion-based planners are effective at modeling complex trajectory distributions, they often suffer from stochastic instabilities and the lack of corrective negative feedback when trained purely with imitation learning. In addition, we propose On-policy Generator Optimization, which converts closed-loop feedback into structured longitudinal optimization signals and progressively shifts the generator toward high-reward trajectory manifolds.
