arXiv Fast Spatial Memory with Elastic Test-Time Training
2026-04-08 · 69 pts · cs.CV · cs.GR · Ziqiao Ma, Xueyang Yu…
📄 New in cs.CV, cs.GR
Large Chunk Test-Time Training (LaCT) has shown strong performance on long-context 3D reconstruction, but its fully plastic inference-time updates remain vulnerable to catastrophic forgetting and overfitting. Based on this updated architecture, we introduce Fast Spatial Memory (FSM), an efficient and scalable model for 4D reconstruction that learns spatiotemporal representations from long observation sequences and renders novel view-time combinations. Extensive experiments show that FSM supports fast adaptation over long sequences and delivers high-quality 3D/4D reconstruction with smaller chunks and mitigating the camera-interpolation shortcut.
